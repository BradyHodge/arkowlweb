About Us

Ok, Game "Studio" is a bit of a reach but it sounds cool, right? We are a couple of university students learning to make games for fun. We all have full time jobs and we don't expect to make money from this adventure. So lets just kick back and have a good time with people we love (or love to hate because they beat us in Mario Cart, THIS IS A CALLOUT POST BAYLEIGH).

Our goal is simple: create good games for good people and have a good time doing it. We believe that games should be accessible, enjoyable, and bring out the best in players. From casual party games to more strategic experiences, we're committed to delivering quality entertainment that creates lasting memories.
